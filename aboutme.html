Who is this girl?

Hi!

I have many names but I usually go by Melody to those who know me in real life meat space.

I'm still figuring out what it is I ought to be doing with this short little life of mine on this fucked up little rock, so I'm throwing myself into as many different pursuits that catch my interest as I can, with as much commitment as I can muster.

My current hobbies include programming, drawing, music composition, voice acting, and writing. I also like playing Celeste and MTG.

I try to avoid forming relationships with internet phantoms, but I do like hearing their whispers when they're nice. If you like anything I've created, or have thoughts about it you'd like to share please shoot me a message with your thoughts!

What is this?

It's my website!

It's more or less just a space for me to express and promote myself on my own terms.

All of the assets and effects on this website (minus the api and fonts I suppose) are exclusively made by me.

The song that plays on this page is me playing a cover of the Outer Wilds theme played on steel tongue drum! ^_^

Also NO AI was used for anything on this site! This website is a testament of my commitment to the laborous process of creativity and expression! No soulless products allowed!

If you wanna know more, feel free to poke around, though the website is still currently majorly under construction

Who am I?

Oh, wow gosh. That's quite an intense question.

Unfortunately I wouldn't know who you are, I'm just the phantom of Melody formed by the words she left here for you to read.

A phantom brought to life by your very own mind, so in some ways I'm a reflection of you as much as I am a reflection of her.

Ideas and thoughts and identity are weird that way. Be careful with the things you expose yourself to because they might become part of you.

Anyway if you really need help finding yourself I suggest maybe writing your thoughts down in a journal or something?

Otherwise, have fun continuing to poke around my site! :3
